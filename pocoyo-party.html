Overview

Pocoyo Party is an educational party game designed for children aged 2-4, built around playful activities, accessible interaction and the recognizable visual language of the Pocoyo universe.

My role

My contribution focused on the bridge between art and implementation: preparing and integrating 3D content in Unity, working on backgrounds, lighting, materials and visual effects, and supporting additional artistic or technical needs during production.
